Summary

Eid-ul-Fitr is a day of joy and gratitude for Muslims. On this day, Muslims thank Allah for granting them the ability to observe the fasts and perform acts of worship during the blessed month of Ramadan.

On Eid, Muslims perform the Eid prayer, give Sadaqat-ul-Fitr, and express love and brotherhood among themselves.

Islam teaches us that we should also include the poor and needy in our happiness.

Therefore, on the day of Eid we should thank Allah, perform good deeds, and treat others kindly.
