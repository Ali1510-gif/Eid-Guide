Amount of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr

Question: What is the amount of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr?

Answer: The amount of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr is one Saa'.

One Saa'

One Saa' is approximately equal to four Mud.

Acceptable Items

This amount can be given in the form of wheat, barley, dates, or raisins.

Hazrat Abdullah ibn Umar (RA) narrated:

The Messenger of Allah ﷺ prescribed one Saa' of dates or one Saa' of barley as Sadaqat-ul-Fitr.

(Sahih Bukhari)
