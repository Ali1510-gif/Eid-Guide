Wisdom Behind Eid

Question: What is the wisdom behind celebrating Eid?

Answer: The wisdom behind celebrating Eid is that Muslims express joy for the blessings of Allah and show gratitude to Him.

After observing the fasts and performing worship during the month of Ramadan, Allah has granted Muslims the happiness of Eid.

Eid also serves as a means of creating love, brotherhood, and unity among Muslims.

On this day, Muslims meet one another, exchange greetings, and express happiness.
