Acts of Worship

Some acts of worship and special practices on the day of Eid

Question: Besides acts of worship, what other things should be observed on the day of Eid?

Answer: Apart from worship, the following practices should be observed on the day of Eid.

Hazrat Abu Hurairah (RA) reported that the Messenger of Allah ﷺ said:

"من قام لیلتی العیدین محتسباً لم یمت قلبہ یوم تموت القلوب"

“Whoever stands in worship on the nights of the two Eids seeking reward, his heart will not die on the day when hearts die.”

(Sunan Ibn Majah, Hadith 1782)
Note: If possible, one should spend the night of Eid in worship to gain the special mercy of Allah.

Supplications on the day of Eid

Question: What should be recited on the day of Eid?

Answer: On the day of Eid, one should recite Takbeerat and other remembrances (Azkar) and praise Allah.
