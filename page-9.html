Rak‘ahs of the Eid Prayer

Question: How many Rak‘ahs are there in the Eid prayer?

Answer: The Eid prayer consists of two Rak‘ahs.

This prayer is performed in congregation, and it includes additional Takbeers.

Hazrat Abdullah ibn Umar (RA) narrated:

The Messenger of Allah ﷺ used to perform two Rak‘ahs for the prayers of Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha.

(Sahih Bukhari)

Additional Takbeers in Eid Prayer

Question: How many Takbeers are there in the Eid prayer?

Answer: There are six additional Takbeers in the Eid prayer.

1

Three Takbeers in the first Rak‘ah

2

Three Takbeers in the second Rak‘ah
