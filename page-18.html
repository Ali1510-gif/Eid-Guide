Recommended Acts on the Day of Eid

Question: What acts are recommended on the day of Eid?

Answer: The following acts are recommended on the day of Eid:

Taking a bath (Ghusl)
Using Miswak
Wearing good clothes
Applying perfume
Going to the Eid prayer ground on foot
Reciting Takbeerat on the way

Hazrat Ali (RA) narrated that:

Taking a bath on the day of Eid is recommended (Mustahab).
