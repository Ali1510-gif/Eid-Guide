When should Sadaqat-ul-Fitr be given

Question: When should Sadaqat-ul-Fitr be given?

Answer: Sadaqat-ul-Fitr should be given before the Eid-ul-Fitr prayer.

If someone gives Sadaqat-ul-Fitr after the Eid prayer, the charity will still count as a normal charity but it will not carry the reward of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr.

Hazrat Abdullah ibn Abbas (RA) narrated that the Prophet ﷺ said:

“Whoever gives Sadaqat-ul-Fitr before the Eid prayer, it is accepted as Fitr charity, and whoever gives it after the prayer, it is just a charity among other charities.”

(Sunan Abu Dawood)
