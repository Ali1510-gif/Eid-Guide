Method of Performing Eid Prayer

Question: How is the Eid prayer performed?

Answer: The Eid prayer consists of two Rak‘ahs and is performed in congregation.

1

First Rak‘ah

In the first Rak‘ah, the Imam says the opening Takbeer (Takbeer-e-Tahrima) and folds his hands, then recites the Sana. After that, three additional Takbeers are said, raising the hands with each Takbeer.

Then the Imam recites Surah Al-Fatiha and another Surah, followed by Ruku and Sujood, completing the first Rak‘ah.

2

Second Rak‘ah

In the second Rak‘ah, after reciting Surah Al-Fatiha and another Surah, three additional Takbeers are said. Then Ruku is performed, and the prayer is completed.
