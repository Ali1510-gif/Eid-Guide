The Night of Eid (Chand Raat)

Step 0: The Most Important Preparation

Chand Raat is actually the night to receive our “Eidi”...

Question: Is there any special virtue?

Yes, certainly...

The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said:

"من قام ليلتي العيدين محتسباً لله لم يمت قلبه يوم تموت القلوب۔"
"Whoever stands in worship on the nights of the two Eids, seeking reward from Allah, his heart will not die on the day when hearts will die."
(Sunan Ibn Majah, 1782)
