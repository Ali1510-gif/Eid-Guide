Fasting on Eid

Question: Is it permissible to fast on the day of Eid?

Answer: No. It is forbidden to fast on the days of Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha.

Hazrat Abu Saeed Al-Khudri (RA) narrated:

نَهَى النَّبِيُّ ﷺ عَنْ صَوْمِ يَوْمِ الْفِطْرِ وَيَوْمِ النَّحْرِ۔

“The Prophet ﷺ forbade fasting on the day of Eid-ul-Fitr and the day of Eid-ul-Adha.”

(Sahih Bukhari, Hadith 1991)

Sunnahs and Recommended Acts on Eid-ul-Fitr

Question: What Sunnahs and recommended acts should be observed on Eid-ul-Fitr?

Answer: The following Sunnahs should be observed:

  • Taking a bath (Ghusl)
  • Wearing good clothes
  • Applying perfume
  • Going for Eid prayer
  • Reciting Takbeerat on the way to the prayer
