Who should receive Sadaqat-ul-Fitr

Question: To whom can Sadaqat-ul-Fitr be given?

Answer: Sadaqat-ul-Fitr can be given to those who are eligible for Zakat.

This includes the poor, the needy, and those in financial hardship.

The Qur’an states:

إِنَّمَا الصَّدَقَاتُ لِلْفُقَرَاءِ وَالْمَسَاكِينِ

“Indeed, charities are for the poor and the needy.”

(Surah At-Tawbah)
Therefore, Sadaqat-ul-Fitr should be given to people who are truly in need.
