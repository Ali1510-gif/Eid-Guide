Who is obligated to give Sadaqat-ul-Fitr

Question: On whom is Sadaqat-ul-Fitr obligatory?

Answer: Sadaqat-ul-Fitr is obligatory on every Muslim who possesses Nisab (minimum required wealth).

  • This means a person who has wealth beyond their basic needs.
  • Every person who meets the Nisab requirement must give Sadaqat-ul-Fitr on their own behalf and on behalf of their minor children.

Hazrat Abdullah ibn Umar (RA) narrated that the Prophet ﷺ said:

“Sadaqat-ul-Fitr is obligatory upon every Muslim.”

(Sahih Bukhari)
