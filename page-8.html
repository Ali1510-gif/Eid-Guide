Ruling of Eid Prayer

Question: What is the ruling of the Eid prayer?

Answer: The Eid prayer is obligatory (Wajib).

According to the Hanafi scholars, the prayers of Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha are obligatory.

Hazrat Abdullah ibn Abbas (RA) narrated that the Prophet ﷺ said:

“The prayer of Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha is obligatory upon Muslims.”

The Eid prayer is an important act of worship in which Muslims gather together.

Time of Eid Prayer

Question: When does the time of Eid prayer begin?

Answer: The time of Eid prayer begins after sunrise and continues until before noon (Zawal).
