Definition of Eid

Question: What is meant by Eid?

Answer: Eid means the return of happiness...

In the Qur'an it is mentioned:

“And do not be excessive. Indeed, He does not like those who commit excess.”

In a Hadith, the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said:

“Every nation has its festival, and this is our festival.”
