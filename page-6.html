Etiquettes that should be observed

Question: What etiquettes should be observed on the day of Eid?

Answer: The following etiquettes should be observed on the day of Eid:

  • Wake up early in the morning
  • Take a bath (Ghusl)
  • Wear good clothes
  • Apply perfume
  • Go for the Eid prayer
  • Recite Takbeerat on the way

Hazrat Abdullah bin Umar (RA) narrated:

"The Messenger of Allah ﷺ used to go to the Eid prayer ground on foot on the day of Eid."

(Sunan Ibn Majah)

"And he used to return by a different route."

(Sahih Bukhari)
