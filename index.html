A Beautiful Tradition of the Companions

The Sunnah Greeting of Eid

تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ

Taqabbalallāhu minnā wa minkum

May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you.

When the companions of the Prophet (PBUH) met on the day of Eid, they would say this to each other as a beautiful prayer of acceptance.
