Things to Avoid on the Day of Eid

Question: What things should be avoided on the day of Eid?

Answer: The following things should be avoided on the day of Eid:

  • Wasting money unnecessarily
  • Committing sinful acts
  • Hurting others
  • Wasting time
Muslims should celebrate Eid with happiness but must ensure that they do not perform any act that goes against Islamic teachings.
