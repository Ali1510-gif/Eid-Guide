Benefits of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr

Question: What are the benefits of giving Sadaqat-ul-Fitr?

Answer: There are several benefits of giving Sadaqat-ul-Fitr:

  • It purifies the fasting person from mistakes and shortcomings in their fasting.
  • It helps the poor and needy.
  • It allows poor people to share in the happiness of Eid.

Hazrat Abdullah ibn Abbas (RA) narrated that the Prophet ﷺ said:

“Sadaqat-ul-Fitr is a means of purification for the fasting person from idle talk and sins, and it is food for the needy.”

(Sunan Abu Dawood)
