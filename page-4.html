Sunnah of Eid Morning

Page 4 of 21

On the day of Eid, it is Sunnah to wake up early, take a bath...

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ used to eat a few dates before going to the Eid prayer.

Anas bin Malik (RA) reported:

“The Messenger of Allah ﷺ would not go out on the morning of Eid-ul-Fitr until he had eaten some dates.”

(Sahih Bukhari, Hadith 964)

Abu Saeed Al-Khudri (RA) narrated:

“The Messenger of Allah ﷺ would not perform any prayer before the Eid prayer.”

(Sunan Ibn Majah, Hadith 1293)
