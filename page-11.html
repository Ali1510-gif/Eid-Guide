Khutbah after the Eid Prayer

Question: Is a sermon delivered after the Eid prayer?

Answer: Yes, a sermon (Khutbah) is delivered after the Eid prayer.

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ used to deliver a Khutbah after the Eid prayer and advise the people.

Hazrat Jabir ibn Abdullah (RA) narrated:

On the day of Eid, after performing the prayer, the Prophet ﷺ stood and delivered a sermon to the people.

(Sahih Bukhari)
Listening to the Khutbah is Sunnah, and Muslims should listen to it quietly and attentively.
