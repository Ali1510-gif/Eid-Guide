Giving greetings on Eid

Question: Is it correct to greet each other on the day of Eid?

Answer: Yes, it is permissible to greet each other on the day of Eid.

The Companions (RA) used to say to each other on Eid:

تَقَبَّلَ اللّٰهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ

“Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum.”

Meaning: May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you.

Islamic scholars have also considered exchanging greetings on Eid to be permissible.

Expressing happiness on Eid

On the day of Eid, one should express happiness and treat their family members kindly.

However, it should be ensured that the expression of happiness does not involve any sinful or inappropriate actions.
